# 🧬 Legendary Pokémon Classification Analysis

## Database and Source

Custom Pokémon relational database (3NF) built from Kaggle dataset

[https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ceebloop/pokmon-dataset-2024](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ceebloop/pokmon-dataset-2024)

---

## Overview

This project presents a comparative analysis of **legendary vs. non-legendary Pokémon** using T-SQL. The goal is to evaluate differences in battle performance, structural characteristics, and trading card market value.

---

## Brief Description

Pokémon is a globally recognized franchise featuring creatures with unique stats, abilities, and types. This project transforms a single-table dataset into a normalized relational database and performs a comprehensive analysis of Pokémon classifications.

The study focuses on comparing performance metrics, structural attributes (types, abilities, egg groups), and economic factors such as card rarity and pricing. It also identifies high-performing Pokémon and pricing outliers to better understand how strength and market value are related.

---

## Objectives

* Compare battle stats between legendary and non-legendary Pokémon
* Analyze structural differences (types, abilities, egg groups)
* Evaluate relationships between stats and card prices
* Identify top-performing Pokémon and high-value card outliers
* Examine dataset distribution and its impact on averages
